Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies

Last Updated: January 1, 2025
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

CashifyGCmart uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and provide personalized content and advertisements.

Key Benefits: Cookies help us remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and improve our service based on how you use our website.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for our website to function properly and cannot be disabled:

  • Authentication: Keep you logged in during your session
  • Security: Protect against cross-site request forgery and other security threats
  • Form Data: Remember information you've entered in forms
  • Load Balancing: Distribute traffic across our servers

Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance functionality and personalization:

  • Preferences: Remember your settings and choices
  • Language: Store your preferred language
  • Accessibility: Remember accessibility preferences
  • Previous Searches: Improve your experience with relevant suggestions

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website:

  • Traffic Analysis: Monitor which pages are most popular
  • User Behavior: Understand how users navigate our site
  • Performance: Identify and fix technical issues
  • Conversion Tracking: Measure the effectiveness of our services

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements (only with your consent):

  • Personalization: Show ads based on your interests
  • Frequency Capping: Control how often you see the same ad
  • Cross-Device Tracking: Recognize you across different devices
  • Attribution: Measure ad campaign effectiveness

5. How to Control Cookies

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data

Cookie Management Options

  • Accept All: Allow all cookies for full functionality
  • Essential Only: Only allow necessary cookies
  • Custom Settings: Choose which types of cookies to accept
  • Block All: Disable all cookies (may affect site functionality)

Opt-Out Tools

  • Google Analytics: Browser Opt-out Add-on
  • Industry Opt-out: Digital Advertising Alliance
  • Do Not Track: Enable in your browser preferences
Important: Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality and your user experience.
