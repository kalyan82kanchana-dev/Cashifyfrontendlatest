General Questions

What is CashifyGCmart?
CashifyGCmart is a trusted online platform that allows you to sell your unused or unwanted gift cards for cash. We offer competitive rates, secure transactions, and fast same-day payments to help you turn your gift cards into money you can actually use.
How does CashifyGCmart work?
The process is simple:
  • Get a Quote: Use our Rate Calculator to see what your gift card is worth
  • Submit Your Card: Fill out our secure form with your card details
  • Get Verified: Our team reviews your submission (usually within 2-4 hours)
  • Get Paid: Receive your payment via your preferred method on the same day
Is CashifyGCmart safe and legitimate?
Yes, absolutely. CashifyGCmart is a legitimate business that has been helping customers sell their gift cards safely for years. We use SSL encryption to protect your data, have secure payment processing, and maintain transparent business practices. We're trusted by thousands of customers nationwide.

Rates and Payments

What rates do you offer for gift cards?
Our rates vary by brand and condition but are highly competitive in the market. Popular brands like Amazon, Apple, and Best Buy can get up to 92% of face value with receipt. Use our Rate Calculator to get an instant quote for your specific gift card. Rates depend on:
  • Gift card brand and popularity
  • Whether you have the original receipt
  • Card condition (new, lightly used, or partially used)
  • Current market demand
What payment methods do you accept?
We offer several convenient payment options:
  • PayPal: Fast and secure digital payments
  • Zelle: Direct bank transfers
  • Cash App: Popular mobile payment platform
  • Bitcoin: Cryptocurrency payments for tech-savvy users
All payments are processed the same day once your submission is approved.
How fast will I get paid?
Same-day payments! Once your gift card is verified and approved (typically within 2-4 hours), payment is processed immediately. Most customers receive their money within a few hours of submission. Note that payments submitted after 8 PM EST may be processed the next business day.
Is there a minimum gift card value?
Yes, we have a minimum value requirement of $50 per gift card. This helps us maintain efficient processing while ensuring worthwhile transactions for both parties. Cards valued below $50 cannot be processed through our platform.

Accepted Cards and Requirements

What gift cards do you accept?
We accept over 100+ popular gift card brands including:
  • Retail: Amazon, Target, Walmart, Best Buy, Home Depot
  • Fashion: Nike, Adidas, Macy's, Nordstrom
  • Technology: Apple, Google Play, iTunes, Steam
  • Food & Dining: Starbucks, DoorDash, Uber Eats
  • Prepaid Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express
Check our Accepted Gift Cards page for the complete list.
Do you accept partially used gift cards?
Yes, we do accept partially used gift cards! However, the rate will be lower than unused cards. When you submit your card, make sure to:
  • Select "Partially Used" as the condition
  • Enter the current remaining balance (not the original amount)
  • Provide clear photos showing the remaining balance
The remaining balance must still meet our $50 minimum requirement.
Do I need the original receipt?
No, the receipt is not required, but having it can increase your payout rate significantly. Gift cards with receipts typically get higher rates (up to 92%) because they provide additional verification and reduce fraud risk. Cards without receipts are still accepted but at slightly lower rates (typically 70-78%).

Process and Support

How long does the verification process take?
Our verification process is typically completed within 2-4 hours during business hours. The process involves:
  • Reviewing your submitted information for accuracy
  • Verifying gift card authenticity and balance
  • Checking for any fraud indicators
  • Preparing your payment
You'll receive email updates throughout the process.
What happens if my gift card is rejected?
If your gift card doesn't pass verification, we'll notify you via email with the specific reason. Common reasons include:
  • Insufficient balance or card already used
  • Card brand not currently accepted
  • Invalid or unreadable card information
  • Suspicious activity or fraud indicators
In most cases, you can resubmit with corrected information or better photos.

Can I track my submission status?
Yes! When you submit your gift card, you'll receive a unique reference number. You can use this reference number to:

  • Contact customer support for specific inquiries

Keep your reference number safe until your transaction is complete.
