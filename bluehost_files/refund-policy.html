Refund Policy

Understanding our refund and cancellation terms

Last Updated: January 1, 2025
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Overview

At CashifyGCmart, we strive to provide clear and fair refund terms for all our gift card exchange services. This Refund Policy explains your rights and our obligations regarding refunds, cancellations, and disputes.

By using our services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Refund Policy.

2. Nature of Our Service

CashifyGCmart operates as a gift card exchange platform where:

  • We purchase valid gift cards from customers at competitive market rates
  • Once a transaction is completed, the gift card becomes our property
  • We provide immediate cash payments through various digital payment methods
  • All transactions are considered final upon successful verification and payment

3. Cancellation Policy

Before Verification

You may cancel your gift card submission at any time before we begin the verification process. To cancel:

  • Contact our support team immediately at support@cashifygcmart.com
  • Include your reference number and reason for cancellation
  • Cancellation requests must be received within 1 hour of submission
  • No fees apply for pre-verification cancellations

During Verification

Once verification has begun, cancellation may not be possible if:

  • We have already confirmed the gift card balance
  • The gift card has been partially or fully processed
  • Third-party verification services have been engaged
Important: Cancellation requests during active verification will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis. Processing fees may apply if significant resources have been utilized.

4. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be issued in the following circumstances:

Eligible Scenarios

  • System Error: Technical issues on our platform that prevent proper processing
  • Incorrect Payment Amount: If we paid less than the agreed amount due to our error
  • Service Failure: If we are unable to complete the transaction due to internal issues
  • Fraudulent Activity: If unauthorized access to your account resulted in unwanted transactions

Non-Eligible Scenarios

  • Change of mind after successful transaction completion
  • Gift card rejection due to insufficient balance or invalidity
  • Rate changes after quote acceptance
  • Payment method issues on the customer's end
  • Misrepresentation of gift card details by the customer

5. Refund Process

How to Request a Refund

  1. Contact Support: Email support@cashifygcmart.com within 48 hours of the transaction
  2. Provide Details: Include your reference number, transaction details, and reason for the refund request
  3. Documentation: Attach any relevant screenshots or evidence supporting your claim
  4. Review Process: Our team will review your request within 2-3 business days

Refund Timeline

  • Review Period: 2-3 business days to assess eligibility
  • Processing Time: 3-5 business days once approved
  • Payment Method: Refunds are issued to the original payment method when possible
  • Alternative Payment: If original method is unavailable, we'll contact you for alternatives

6. Partial Refunds

In certain circumstances, partial refunds may be issued:

  • Processing Fees: If significant processing costs have been incurred
  • Gift Card Devaluation: If the gift card value has decreased between submission and cancellation
  • Third-Party Costs: Fees charged by verification services or payment processors
  • Administrative Costs: Reasonable fees for transaction processing and handling

All partial refund calculations will be clearly explained and documented in your refund communication.

7. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with a refund decision:

  1. Internal Review: Request an escalated review within 7 days of the initial decision
  2. Additional Documentation: Provide any new evidence or information
  3. Management Review: Senior management will review the case within 5 business days
  4. Final Decision: Our final decision will be communicated in writing
Alternative Dispute Resolution: For unresolved disputes, we encourage mediation through recognized consumer protection agencies before pursuing legal action.

8. Exceptions and Special Cases

High-Value Transactions

Transactions over $1,000 may be subject to:

  • Extended verification periods
  • Additional documentation requirements
  • Modified cancellation windows
  • Enhanced security protocols

Suspected Fraud

If we suspect fraudulent activity:

  • Transactions may be held pending investigation
  • Additional verification may be required
  • Law enforcement may be contacted if necessary
  • Refunds may be processed differently to protect all parties
